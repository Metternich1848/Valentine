Saint Valentine's Day

Be My Valentine 

In order to build a powerful army, Emperor Claudius II prohibited the marriage of young Roman men. However, Bishop Valentine defied the cruel decree of the monarch and conducted secret weddings for the young soldiers. On February 14th, 270 AD, Valentine was put to death because of opposing the order of the king.

Prior to his execution, Valentine wrote a letter to his sweetheart, signing it "Your Valentine".

Why Do We Give Red Roses on Valentine's Day?

Red roses are considered the emblem of passion and romantic love. As per Greek mythology, the very first red rose was created when Aphrodite, the Goddess of Love, injured herself by accident and her blood turned a white rose into a red one.

Why Do We Give Chocolate on Valentine's Day?

Chocolate is said to represent profound love and deep affection. According to several scientific studies, chocolate has aphrodisiac properties which raises one’s energy and desire levels.

Which countries banned Valentine's Day?

The celebration of Valentine’s Day is forbidden or restricted in some Muslim nations, including Indonesia, Iran, Malaysia, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Uzbekistan.
